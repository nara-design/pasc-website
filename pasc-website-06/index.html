Welcome to PASC

We support, promote and advocate for practitioners of Pan-Asian descent in New Zealand’s screen sector. We refer to the United Nations definition of Pan-Asia which includes all of Asia as well as the Middle East. PASC’s mission is to see more stories made by and about our Pan-Asian communities by supporting the careers of Pan-Asian practitioners.
